# Central Dogma of Molecular Biology

## Notes
- General Transformations:
    1. DNA -> DNA
    2. DNA -> RNA
    3. RNA -> Protein
- Special Transformations (only are minor exceptions, generally don't occur):
    1. RNA -> RNA (virii)
    2. RNA -> DNA
    3. DNA -> Protein
- Might never occur
    1. Protein -> Protein
    2. Protein -> DNA
    3. Protein -> RNA
